Black Bean Soup Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cans black beans (400g each), rinsed and drained
  • 2 cups vegetable or chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional toppings: sour cream, chopped cilantro, lime wedges, shredded cheese

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  2. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic. Saut√© until the onion becomes translucent.
  3. Add the black beans, vegetable or chicken broth, ground cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper. Stir well.
  4. Bring the soup to a boil, then reduce the heat to low. Let it simmer for about 15-20 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.
  5. Use an immersion blender or transfer a portion of the soup to a blender, blend until smooth, then return it to the pot. This step is optional, but it helps to thicken the soup.
  6. Taste the soup and adjust the seasoning if needed.
  7. Serve hot, garnished with optional toppings if desired.
