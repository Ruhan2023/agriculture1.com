Cucumber Smoothie Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 large cucumber, peeled and chopped
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 1/2 cup fresh mint leaves
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • 1 cup ice cubes

Instructions:

  1. In a blender, combine the chopped cucumber, yogurt, mint leaves, honey, and lime juice.
  2. Blend on high speed until the mixture is smooth and well combined.
  3. Add the ice cubes to the blender and continue blending until the smoothie reaches your desired consistency.
  4. Taste the smoothie and adjust the sweetness or tanginess by adding more honey or lime juice if desired.
  5. Pour the cucumber smoothie into glasses and serve immediately. You can garnish with additional mint leaves if you like.
