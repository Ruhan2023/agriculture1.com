Bean Salad Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 can mixed beans (kidney beans, chickpeas, black beans, etc.), rinsed and drained
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1 small red onion, finely chopped
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • Cherry tomatoes, halved
  • Fresh parsley or cilantro, chopped
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the mixed beans, diced bell pepper, chopped red onion, diced cucumber, halved cherry tomatoes, and chopped parsley or cilantro.
  2. In a separate small bowl, whisk together the lemon juice, extra-virgin olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  3. Pour the dressing over the bean salad and toss well to combine.
  4. Adjust the seasoning if needed.
  5. Let the salad sit for at least 15 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together before serving.
