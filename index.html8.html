Cucumber Salad Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 large cucumbers
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons fresh dill, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons white vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Slice the cucumbers thinly and place them in a bowl.
  2. Add the sliced red onion and chopped dill to the bowl.
  3. In a separate small bowl, whisk together the white vinegar, olive oil, sugar, salt, and pepper.
  4. Pour the dressing over the cucumbers and toss well to coat.
  5. Cover the bowl and refrigerate for at least 1 hour to allow the flavors to meld.
  6. Serve chilled and enjoy!
