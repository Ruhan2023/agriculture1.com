Red Tomato Sauce Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes (400g)
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: sugar (to balance acidity)

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large saucepan over medium heat.
  2. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic. Saut√© until the onion becomes translucent.
  3. Add the crushed tomatoes, dried basil, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Stir well.
  4. Bring the sauce to a simmer, then reduce the heat to low. Let it cook for about 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Taste the sauce and adjust the seasoning as desired. If the sauce is too acidic, you can add a pinch of sugar to balance the flavors.
  6. Once the sauce has thickened, it is ready to be used in your favorite recipes.
